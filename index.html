About Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi, the epitome of non-violence and peace, is revered as the Father of the Nation in India. His philosophy of truth and non-violence (Satyagraha) inspired movements for civil rights and freedom across the world.

His leadership during India's independence movement against British colonial rule led to the adoption of non-violent resistance, influencing countless individuals and leaders globally.
